Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Games Arc collects information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact information when you communicate with us via Telegram, Discord, or email
  • Game preferences and download history
  • Payment information for premium game purchases (processed through third-party services)
  • Feedback and support requests

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Download statistics and game preferences
  • Device information (operating system, screen resolution)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process premium game purchases
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Send updates about new games and features
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of Games Arc and our users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website (under confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure payment processing through trusted third-party services
  • Limited access to personal information to authorized personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services:

  • Telegram for communication
  • Discord for community interaction
  • Payment processors for premium game purchases
  • Download hosting services (Akira Box, Media Fire, etc.)

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your information
  • Right to object to processing of your information
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us
